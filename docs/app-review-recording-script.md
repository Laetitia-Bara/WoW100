# App Review Recording Script - WoW100%

Use a physical iPhone on the latest available iOS version. Start recording before launching the app.

## Before recording

- Install the exact build submitted to App Store Connect or the new build you will submit.
- Prepare a Battle.net review/demo account if you decide to provide credentials to Apple.
- Disable two-factor authentication on the demo account, or Apple may be unable to log in.
- Make sure the account has at least one World of Warcraft character, otherwise the character selection screen will not demonstrate the app well.
- If the Battle.net flow cannot be shared with credentials, still show it in the video with your own account, but do not reveal private information you do not want Apple to keep.

## Video flow

1. Launch WoW100% from the iPhone home screen.
2. Show the main dashboard and the total progress card.
3. Tap the category filters and show achievements, mounts, and pets.
4. Open one expansion card.
5. Use the search field in the planner.
6. Toggle "Afficher uniquement les manquants".
7. Toggle "Masquer les indisponibles".
8. Check one item manually as obtained, then show that progress changes.
9. Tap the external link icon on an item to show the Wowhead reference opens outside the app.
10. Return to WoW100%.
11. Tap "Connexion".
12. Complete the Battle.net OAuth flow.
13. Select a character.
14. Show the dashboard with character name, realm, faction, level, achievement points, and progress.
15. Open "Mes personnages" and show character selection.
16. Return to the dashboard.
17. Tap the legal information icon and open the privacy/legal screens.
18. Return and tap logout to show disconnection clears the Battle.net session inside the app.

## App Store Connect checklist

- Add the video link or attachment in the reply to App Review.
- Paste the text from `docs/apple-review-notes.md` into App Review Information > Notes.
- Fill in the tested devices and iOS versions.
- Add demo credentials if you are comfortable providing a dedicated Battle.net review account.
- Resubmit the app version.
